About Creative Kids Music

Music is a language—let your child speak it.

Imagine · Jam · Breathe · Bloom

Our Philosophy

"Music is too important to be boring"

At Creative Kids Music, we believe that traditional music education often misses the mark. Too many programs focus on perfection over passion, technique over creativity, and reading over doing. We're different. We believe that music is a language—and like any language, children learn it best through exploration, play, and meaningful expression.

Every Child is Musical

There's no such thing as a "non-musical" child. Some children sing, some move, some drum on everything in sight—but all children have music within them. Our job is to help them find their unique musical voice.

Joy Leads to Excellence

When children love what they're doing, they naturally want to do it well. We create an environment where excellence emerges from enthusiasm, not from pressure or competition.

Creation from Day One

Why wait years before letting children create their own music? From their very first day, our students improvise, compose, and express themselves musically. They're not just learning music—they're making it.

Community Over Competition

Music is meant to be shared. Our small groups create a supportive community where children inspire each other, collaborate on projects, and discover the joy of making music together.

Why Creative Kids Music?

In a world of screens and schedules, music offers children something irreplaceable: a chance to create, express, and connect. Here's what makes our program special:

✓ Multi-Instrument Foundation

Unlike traditional lessons that lock children into one instrument, we expose them to piano, ukulele, percussion, and voice—building a complete musical foundation.

✓ Small Group Magic

With a maximum of 12 students per group, children get individual attention while experiencing the joy and motivation of learning with peers.

✓ Creative Curriculum

Our curriculum balances structure with freedom, technique with creativity, and individual growth with group collaboration.

✓ Age-Appropriate Excellence

We maintain high standards while honoring where children are developmentally. Excellence looks different at every age, and we celebrate each milestone.

✓ Inclusive Community

Music is for everyone. We welcome all children regardless of experience, learning style, or background. Our scholarships ensure financial constraints don't limit access.

✓ Convenience for Families

After-school hours, optional extended care, and potential school pickup make it easy for working families to give their children this enriching experience.
