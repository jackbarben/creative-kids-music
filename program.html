The Creative Kids Music Program

A comprehensive musical journey designed to nurture complete musicians

What Makes Our Program Special

Our revolutionary approach combines the best of traditional music education with modern creative pedagogy. Every child explores multiple instruments, learns to read and write music, and creates their own musical expressions from day one.

A Typical Day at Creative Kids Music

Time Activity What We're Learning
3:00-3:30 Arrival & Outdoor Play Social connection, transition time, physical activity
3:30-3:45 Musical Warm-Up Circle singing, call and response, rhythm games
3:45-4:15 Instrument Exploration Rotating through piano, drums, and voice stations
4:15-4:30 Music Theory Through Play Solfege, note reading, rhythm patterns, theory games
4:30-5:00 Creative Time Free improvisation, soloing, composition, sit spots
5:00-5:20 Ensemble Practice Playing together, listening skills, performance prep
5:20-5:30 Reflection & Closing Sharing discoveries, setting goals, celebration
5:30-6:00 Extended Care (Optional) Free play with instruments, homework time, quiet activities

*Schedule varies by day to keep things fresh and engaging!

Skills Your Child Will Develop

Musical Skills

  • Read standard music notation fluently
  • Play multiple instruments with proper technique
  • Understand chord progressions and harmony
  • Improvise melodies and rhythms confidently
  • Compose original songs and pieces
  • Perform solo and in groups
  • Develop relative pitch and ear training
  • Master rhythm and timing

Life Skills

  • Creative problem-solving
  • Confidence in self-expression
  • Collaboration and teamwork
  • Discipline and practice habits
  • Active listening skills
  • Performance confidence
  • Cultural appreciation
  • Emotional intelligence

Tailored for Every Age

Grades K-2

Focus: Musical exploration through play

  • Circle singing and movement games
  • Introduction to instruments
  • Call and response activities
  • Basic solfege and music symbols
  • Free improvisation and sit spots

Grades 3-4

Focus: Building fundamental skills

  • Note reading fluency
  • Piano chord basics and technique
  • Soloing and improvisation
  • Writing short compositions
  • Ensemble playing and groove

Grades 5-6

Focus: Advanced musicianship

  • Complex rhythms and time signatures
  • Harmony and chord progressions
  • Extended improvisation
  • Arranging and songwriting
  • Performance preparation

Mixed Age Benefits

Focus: Peer learning and mentorship

  • Older students mentor younger ones
  • Natural differentiation
  • Family-like atmosphere
  • Multiple skill levels in ensemble
  • Inspiration from peers

Sharing Their Music

Performance is a natural extension of music-making. We create supportive, low-pressure opportunities for children to share their progress and creativity with others.

Monthly Showcases

Informal gatherings where students share works-in-progress, new compositions, or skills they're developing.

Semester Concerts

More formal performances featuring polished pieces, group numbers, and student compositions.

Community Events

Opportunities to perform at local venues, schools, or community gatherings.

Recording Projects

Create lasting memories with group recording sessions of favorite songs and original compositions.
