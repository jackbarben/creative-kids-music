# Reserve

Parking lot for thoughts and items that aren't getting active attention right now. Below inbox priority, above forgotten.

## How this works

Three-layer flow for capturing and processing thinking:

1. **Inbox** (nas-mcp `creative-kids-music` inbox) — active attention. Things on the short list right now.
2. **Reserve** (this file) — parked. Captured but not getting cycles. Aware of, not active on.
3. **Processed** (`docs/<topic>/<title>.md`) — settled, long-form. Output of thinking, organized by topic.

Items move freely between inbox and reserve as priorities shift. Once an item has been thought through, the result becomes a categorized doc and the inbox/reserve pointer closes.
