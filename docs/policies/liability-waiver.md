# Creative Kids Music - Liability Waiver and Release of Claims

**Version 1.0** | Effective January 1, 2025

## Assumption of Risk and Release of Liability

I, the undersigned parent or legal guardian, hereby acknowledge and agree to the following:

### 1. Nature of Activities

I understand that Creative Kids Music programs include, but are not limited to:
- Playing musical instruments
- Physical movement and dance
- Group activities and collaboration
- Performance opportunities
- Use of audio/visual equipment

I acknowledge that these activities involve physical exertion and carry inherent risks of injury.

### 2. Assumption of Risk

I understand that participation in Creative Kids Music programs involves risks that may result in injury. These risks include, but are not limited to:
- Minor injuries such as cuts, bruises, or strains
- Injuries from physical activity or movement
- Hearing-related issues from musical instruments or equipment
- Injuries from interaction with other participants
- Injuries resulting from equipment use

I voluntarily assume all risks associated with my child's participation in Creative Kids Music programs.

### 3. Release of Liability

In consideration of my child being permitted to participate in Creative Kids Music programs, I hereby release, waive, and forever discharge Creative Kids Music, its instructors, staff, volunteers, agents, and affiliated organizations (including St. Luke's/San Lucas Episcopal Church) from any and all liability, claims, demands, and causes of action arising out of or related to any injury, loss, or damage that may occur during participation.

This release applies to all claims, whether caused by the negligence of Creative Kids Music or otherwise, except for claims arising from gross negligence or intentional misconduct.

### 4. Medical Authorization

In case of emergency, I authorize Creative Kids Music staff to obtain emergency medical treatment for my child. I understand that I will be notified as soon as reasonably possible in the event of such emergency.

### 5. Physical Ability

I certify that my child is physically able to participate in the program activities. I have disclosed any relevant medical conditions, allergies, or special needs on the registration form.

### 6. Photo/Video Use

I understand that photos and videos may be taken during programs. My preferences for media use are indicated separately on the Media Release form.

### 7. Rules and Conduct

I agree that my child will follow all rules, guidelines, and instructions provided by Creative Kids Music staff. I understand that disruptive or dangerous behavior may result in dismissal from the program without refund.

### 8. Indemnification

I agree to indemnify and hold harmless Creative Kids Music and its staff from any claims, damages, or expenses (including attorney fees) arising from my child's participation in the program.

### 9. Governing Law

This agreement shall be governed by the laws of the State of Washington. Any disputes shall be resolved in Clark County, Washington.

### 10. Severability

If any provision of this agreement is found to be unenforceable, the remaining provisions shall continue in full force and effect.
