# Creative Kids Music - Medical Authorization

**Version 1.0** | Effective January 1, 2025

*Applies to Summer Camp registrations*

---

## Emergency Medical Authorization

This authorization allows Creative Kids Music staff to obtain emergency medical treatment for your child in the event of a medical emergency during camp activities.

### Authorization

In the event of a medical emergency, I hereby authorize Creative Kids Music staff to:

1. **Seek Emergency Care**: Contact emergency services (911) and transport my child to the nearest medical facility if necessary.

2. **Consent to Treatment**: Authorize emergency medical treatment, including but not limited to:
   - First aid and basic medical care
   - Emergency room treatment
   - Diagnostic procedures
   - Surgery, if deemed immediately necessary by medical professionals

3. **Share Medical Information**: Provide medical professionals with the health information I have disclosed on the registration form, including allergies, medical conditions, and current medications.

### Notification

I understand that Creative Kids Music will:
- Contact me (or my designated emergency contact) as soon as reasonably possible in any medical situation
- Attempt to reach me before any non-emergency medical decisions are made
- Document all medical incidents and report them to me

### Medical Information

I certify that the medical information provided on the registration form is accurate and complete, including:
- Known allergies
- Current medications
- Medical conditions
- Special needs or accommodations

I agree to update this information if it changes before or during the camp.

### Insurance

I understand that Creative Kids Music does not provide medical insurance for participants. I am responsible for any medical expenses incurred on behalf of my child. I agree to provide my insurance information to medical providers as needed.

### Limitations

This authorization:
- Is valid only for the duration of the summer camp program
- Does not authorize experimental treatments
- Does not authorize procedures that can safely wait for parental consent
- Shall be interpreted in accordance with Washington State law

### Release

I release Creative Kids Music and its staff from liability for:
- Good faith decisions made in emergency situations
- Following my child's stated medical needs
- Actions taken by emergency medical professionals

This release does not apply to gross negligence or willful misconduct.

---

## Required for Camp Participation

This medical authorization is required for all Summer Camp participants. Without this authorization, your child cannot participate in the camp program.

---

## Acknowledgment

By checking the medical authorization checkbox on the camp registration form, I acknowledge that:

1. I have read and understand this Medical Authorization
2. I am the parent or legal guardian of the child(ren) being registered
3. I authorize Creative Kids Music staff to obtain emergency medical treatment for my child
4. I have provided accurate medical information on the registration form
5. I understand my child cannot participate in camp without this authorization
